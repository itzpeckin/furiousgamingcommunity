# Franchise HQ — TC-006 Committee Review

TC-006 adds a confidential, role-controlled Trade Committee workflow on top of the permanent negotiation foundation.

## Included
- Dedicated committee review snapshot with package, cap, premium-asset, and submitted-version context.
- Five-member confidential ballot with approve, reject, and abstain options.
- Required rationale for rejections and abstentions.
- Ballots can be updated until a final threshold is reached.
- Automatic finalization at three approvals or three rejections.
- Owners see review progress only; individual votes and comments remain hidden.
- Commissioner has read-only oversight and receives the private rejection-message handoff.
- Committee members receive review notifications when an accepted trade enters the queue.
- Approved trades publish final terms to League News while negotiation details remain private.
